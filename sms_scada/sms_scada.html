SMS SCADA

A Remote Relay and Data Acquisition

Overview:

(This page is under construction...) This SMS SCADA or text message based Supervisory Control and Data Acquisition was originally designed to act as a remote vehicle tracking platform that solved the issues existing market solutions struggled with. Communication range for these devices on the market were limited to a county-by-county basis in where RF transceivers had been installed. My design uses the cellular grid to exponentially increase the coverage for the device. Remotely the user can request a coordinate location and disable/enable the vehicle with a code. This is crucial for combatting vehicle theft where the user can disable there car without having to be inside such as the case in a car-jacking scenario.
